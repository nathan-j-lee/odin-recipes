Protein Shake

Description

One of my goto meals from a heavy workout or just a quick meal on the go!

Very quick to make as most of the prep work is from buying the powder.

You can also add whatever ingredient you want into the shake for a better taste/texture

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder of your choice (I would recommend a vanilla or chocolate flavor as they are the most common)
  • Water (or some kind of milk for texture)
  • Any fruit you would like (or none to keep it simple)

Steps

  1. Add liquid of your choice to blender or shaker cup
  2. Add a scoop of protein powder to blender or shaker cup
  3. Optional: (If you are using a blender) Add fruits to blender
  4. Run blender until smooth or shake cup until smooth
  5. Enjoy!
