Ribeye Steak

Description

A great choice for meatlovers like myself.

Relatively simple to make, but still takes practice to master.

An amazing savory taste when cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Garlic (prefferably cloves)
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional: Vegetables to cook such as asparagus or zucchini

Steps

  1. If steak is frozen, leave out until room temperature (or run steak in lukewarm water inside bag until thawed)
  2. Preheat pan to medium-high heat
  3. Pat-dry steak using paper towels
  4. Season steak with salt and pepper on both sides
  5. Add olive oil to pan until evenly coated
  6. Add steak to pan. Cook for 4-6 minutes per side
  7. While steak is cooking, crush cloves of garlic
  8. Flip steak over when time passed
  9. Cook steak for one minute less than the same time as before
  10. When time passed, lower heat to medium-low and add garlic, rosemary and butter to pan
  11. Using a spoon, scoop the butter onto the steak and repeat for at least a minute
  12. Turn heat off and remove steak from pan
  13. Let the steak rest for at least two minutes. Cut and enjoy!
